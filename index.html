Empowering Minds through Inquiry & Reflection

Hello and welcome. I am Dr. Kayla Willis, an Associate Professor at MCC. My scholarship focuses on critical pedagogy, historical perspectives in modern society, and developing inclusive curricula that build resilient, lifelong learners.

With over a decade of teaching and research experience, I dedicate my classroom to turning concepts into creative action and equipping students with analytical toolkits to navigate complex structural landscapes.
