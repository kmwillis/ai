💡 The Foundations Spectrum

Educational philosophies exist on a rich spectrum. At one end, some argue that schools exist to preserve historical knowledge, culture, and foundational skills. At the other end, some believe school is an engine for discovery, personal growth, and societal change.

Teacher-Centered Paradigm

Focuses on passing down knowledge, structured curricula, discipline, and core cultural truths.

Student-Centered Paradigm

Focuses on experiences, active experimentation, real-world problems, and social transformation.
