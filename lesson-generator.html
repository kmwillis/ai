Under the Hood: Community College Pedagogical Integrity

            

                

                    
Bloom's Taxonomy Framework

                    

                        The interactive helper provides action verbs classified by cognitive domain. Specifying clear levels of thinking prevents simple regurgitation prompts and ensures assignments match appropriate difficulty tiers (from comprehension up to synthesis).
                    

                

                

                    
Universal Design for Learning (UDL)

                    

                        Community college campuses are home to neurodivergent learners, returning adults, and students with developmental requirements. The accessibility configurations ensure that the AI outputs content that is naturally formatted to conform to Section 508 and WCAG standards.
                    

                

                

                    
Mitigating Generative AI Shortcut-Taking

                    

                        Rather than trying to prohibit AI access, this tool embeds specific "AI-Mitigation" logic that creates activities based on localized context, immediate student-lived experience, scaffolded processes, or physical/oral artifacts that AI engines cannot mimic cleanly.
